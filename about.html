About Sean

I have a passion for computers, technology, and programming. I've recently finished studying a Bachelor's of Information and Communication Technology at the University of the Sunshine Coast and aspire to work as a software developer or programmer.

I have always had a great passion and interest in problem-solving with a rich history in many strategy games like Chess and Poker. To me programming feels like a natural progression from these activities where you are given a problem and must use the resources you currently have to find the best possible solution.

I also have a strong desire for mental health, fitness, and sport. I'm a massive rugby league fan and played throughout high school in representative sides for Manly-Warringah.
